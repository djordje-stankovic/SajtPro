Our Story

It started with a simple question.

Why do so many great businesses have terrible websites?

We walked through Serbia looking at amazing local businesses — bakeries, restaurants, repair shops, salons — and noticed something: most of them either don't have a website, or have one that looks like it was made in 2010.

The truth is, these people do amazing work. It's just that their online presence doesn't show it. And that's where we decided to make a difference.
